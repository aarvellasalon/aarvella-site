1. Appointment Etiquette

To maintain the serene atmosphere of Aarvella, we kindly request that clients arrive 10 minutes prior to their scheduled time.

  • Late Arrivals: Appointments delayed by more than 15 minutes may be subject to rescheduling or a shortened service time to ensure subsequent guests are not impacted.
  • Cancellations: We require 24 hours' notice for all cancellations. Changes made within this window may incur a 50% service fee.

2. Service Consultations & Results

Every transformation begins with a professional consultation. While our artisans strive for perfection, results may vary based on hair history and condition.

  • Patch Testing: Required 48 hours prior for all first-time chemical or color services.
  • Disclosures: Clients must inform stylists of any allergies or medical conditions prior to treatment. Aarvella is not liable for reactions to undisclosed sensitivities.

3. The Aarvella Environment

Aarvella is a sanctuary of luxury. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy regarding harassment or inappropriate behavior toward our staff or other guests.

  • Personal Items: Please safeguard your belongings. The salon is not responsible for lost or damaged personal items.
  • Children: For safety and tranquility, supervised children are welcome; however, we ask that the quiet nature of the space be respected.

4. Payments & Refunds

All prices are subject to change based on hair length, density, and complexity of work. Final quotes are provided during the consultation.

  • Refunds: Services are non-refundable. If you are not entirely satisfied, please contact us within 7 days for a complimentary adjustment.
